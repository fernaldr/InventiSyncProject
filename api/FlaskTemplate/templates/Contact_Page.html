FAQ's

Will my inventory automatically update?

Yes! After inputing your used materials the database automatically updates your total amount!


Am I able to see a history of my used products?

Yes! There is a feature to view your product history that shows what you have used and what you have bought.


How does Quantity checking Work?

Quantity Checking works by automatically sending an email when an item in your inventory gets low. You can determine how low an item

gets to before an email is sent. This helps ensure you will always have what you need!
